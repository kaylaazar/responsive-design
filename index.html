Top 5 Affordable Meals for Students

Easy, delicious, and budget-friendly meals perfect for college life.

5. Grilled Cheese & Tomato Soup

A warm and comforting combo that’s cheap, easy to make, and perfect for cold days. Pair crispy golden bread with creamy tomato soup for the ultimate cozy meal.

4. Meatball Sub

Juicy meatballs in marinara sauce with melted cheese on a toasted sub roll — affordable, filling, and easy to prepare or grab on the go.

3. Shrimp Fried Rice

A quick stir-fry with shrimp, rice, eggs, and veggies — a delicious way to use leftovers and get protein on a student budget.

2. Cheeseburger Sliders

Mini burgers with melted cheese — easy to share, customizable with toppings, and perfect for group meals or quick dinners.

1. Chicken Marsala

An Italian classic with pan-seared chicken and a rich mushroom Marsala wine sauce — surprisingly easy and affordable to make at home.
